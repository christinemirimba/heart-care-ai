Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HeartCareAI's AI-powered cardiovascular assessment platform

How accurate is HeartCareAI's risk assessment?

HeartCareAI's AI algorithms achieve 98%+ accuracy in cardiovascular risk assessment, trained on extensive medical datasets and validated against clinical studies. However, it should complement, not replace, professional medical advice.

Is my health data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption and follow HIPAA compliance standards. Your health data is stored securely and never shared without your explicit consent. We employ bank-level security measures to protect your information.

How long does an assessment take?

The assessment process typically takes 2-3 minutes to complete the health form, and you'll receive your risk score and personalized recommendations within 3 seconds of submission. It's one of the fastest cardiovascular assessments available.

Can I use HeartCareAI without medical training?

Yes! HeartCareAI is designed for everyone. The interface is user-friendly and doesn't require medical expertise. However, interpreting results should always involve healthcare professionals for comprehensive care.

What health parameters do I need to provide?

We need basic health metrics: age, sex, blood pressure, cholesterol levels, maximum heart rate, fasting blood sugar, resting ECG results, exercise tolerance, chest pain type, and ST slope. All measurements are standard and commonly available.

How often should I take an assessment?

We recommend quarterly assessments (every 3-4 months) for tracking trends and monitoring the effectiveness of lifestyle changes. However, you can take assessments as often as needed, especially after significant health or lifestyle changes.

Is HeartCareAI a replacement for doctor visits?

No, HeartCareAI is a screening tool, not a replacement for professional medical care. Our assessments provide insights and recommendations but should always be discussed with healthcare providers for comprehensive evaluation and treatment.

What if my risk score is high?

A high risk score indicates you should consult with a healthcare provider promptly. We'll provide personalized recommendations, but professional medical evaluation is essential for proper diagnosis and treatment planning.

Can I export or share my assessment results?

Yes, you can view and print your assessment history and results from your account. You can share these with healthcare providers to support your medical consultations. Your data remains under your control at all times.

Is HeartCareAI free to use?

HeartCareAI offers both free and premium features. Basic risk assessments are free, while advanced features like detailed reports, trend analysis, and personalized coaching may require a subscription. Check our pricing page for details.
